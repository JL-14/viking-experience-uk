The Viking Experience Packages

The Viking Experience UK offers three set packages designed to give you a flavour of what it was like for the Vikings arriving and settling in the UK. Each package covers a separate aspect of Viking life, including life at sea, life at camp, and the arts and crafts (and games) the Vikings created as part of their everyday life.

There are some elements that are common to all three experiences, such as:

  • Food and cooking -we will cook and eat as the Vikings did -although you are welcome to bring your own food and drink to cater for preferences and dietary requirements)
  • Clothing -you will be provided with a set of Viking-style clothing including a tunic, breeches, headdress, and belts and other accessories
  • Weapons -you will again be provided with Viking weapons appropriate to youractivity (even if murder and pillaging is strictly off the agenda!)
